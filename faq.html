Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Seasonal hours may vary.

Do I need to book tickets in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking tickets online in advance to secure your spot and take advantage of early bird discounts.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free parking for all our guests. There are ample parking spaces near the main entrance.
